# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in HEXSONIC respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and outcomes.
- Respect legal boundaries, especially around copyrighted media.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, doxxing, or threats.
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith collaboration.
- Sharing illegal content, credentials, or private data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject content that violates this policy and may apply temporary or permanent bans.

## Reporting

Please report violations privately via the security/support channels documented in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) and [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).
